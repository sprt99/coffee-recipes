Ingredients
1/3 cup ground coffee
1 cup water
1/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoons instant vanilla pudding mix
2-1/2 cups 2% milk

Directions
Place ground coffee in the coffee filter of a drip coffeemaker. Add water; brew according to manufacturer's directions.
In a small bowl, combine sugar and dry pudding mix. Stir in milk and coffee. Chill until serving.
